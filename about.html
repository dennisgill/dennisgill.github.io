About Me


I am third-year student pursuing my bachelors of science degree in the field of computer science and engineering at the University of California Davis. I am also double majoring in political science.


I’ve been interested in computers and programming since I was in high school. While in high school, I worked in the tech office servicing malfunctioning MacBooks. I replaced hardware components in the machines and loaded operating systems on them.


I’ve held multiple positions at the UC Davis Police Department. Beginning my career there as a AHS Officer, I was quickly promoted to lead supervisor for the student security division. I was placed in-charge for the successful deployment of our campus ride-hailing service. I worked with developers to improve and deploy the application web-wide.


Thus far in college, I’ve taken: Introduction to Programming, Discrete Math, Data Structures, Object-Oriented Programming, Programming in Assembly Language, Computer Architecture, and Databases.
